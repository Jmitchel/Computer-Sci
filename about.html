Favorite Things

One of my favourite things is Rubik's Cubes, yep Rubik's Cubes the puzzle everyone hates because it's "impossible". Well to that I say ha because I can solve it in under a minute and fifteen seconds. However that isn't the only thing I enjoy doing in my freetime, I also enjoy reading books because with books you can learn any subject you could ever want to, that is if there is a book on the subject and you are willing to devote your time to learning that subject but if you want to learn the subject that's no issue.

Hobbies
